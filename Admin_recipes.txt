Chicken Teriyaki
Main Course
4 boneless skinless chicken thighs, 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/4 cup mirin, 1/4 cup sake (or dry sherry/white wine), 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 clove garlic (minced), 1 teaspoon ginger (grated), 1 tablespoon vegetable oil.
Marinate chicken thighs in a mixture of soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, garlic, and ginger for at least 30 minutes; cook marinated chicken in heated vegetable oil over medium-high heat until browned and fully cooked; boil and simmer the reserved marinade until it thickens, then pour over the cooked chicken and toss to coat; serve hot with steamed rice and vegetables.
Club Sandwich
Appetizer
3 slices of toasted bread, 2 slices turkey breast, 1 slice cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato slices, mayonnaise
Layer toasted bread with mayonnaise, lettuce, turkey, tomato, another slice of bread with mayonnaise, cheese, and top with the final slice of bread with mayonnaise; secure with toothpicks and cut into quarters to serve.
 Lasagna
Pasta
Lasagna noodles, ground beef, marinara sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, onion, olive oil, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
Cook lasagna noodles; sauté garlic and onion in olive oil, add ground beef, cook until browned, and mix in marinara sauce; combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning; layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish, repeating layers and ending with mozzarella; bake at 375°F for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
